Our story

Beauty rooted in care

Nestled in the heart of El Nido, Palawan, Primary Salon and Spa is your sanctuary for beauty and relaxation. We bring together skilled professionals and premium products to offer a full suite of services — from precision haircuts to rejuvenating body massages.

Whether you're a local or a traveller discovering paradise, we invite you to slow down, refresh, and leave feeling your absolute best.
